Privacy Policy

This application is a student project created for learning purposes.

What data we collect

  • Username
  • Hashed password
  • Consent record (acceptance of ToS and Privacy Policy, version, and timestamp)
  • Polls you create (question text and answer options)
  • Votes cast on polls (option selected and time of vote, not linked to any user account)

How data is used

The data is used only to allow users to create an account, log in, create and share polls, and record votes on those polls. No data is sold or shared with third parties.

Data storage

All data is stored in a persistent PostgreSQL database hosted on Render. Data is retained until you delete your account or the application is shut down.

User rights

You may delete your account at any time using the "Delete Account" button in the app. This removes your username, password, and consent record from the database. Polls you have created and their associated votes are also deleted.
